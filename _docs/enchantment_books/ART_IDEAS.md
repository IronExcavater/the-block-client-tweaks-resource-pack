# Enchantment Book And Glyph Ideas

## Active Targets

- `veinminer_enchantment:veinminer`
  - Book: start from Re-covered Efficiency, change the center mark into a cracked ore-vein branch.
  - Palette: iron gray pages, cyan/teal vein lines, small pickaxe notch.
  - Glyph: 9px cluster of three ore blocks connected by a diagonal crack.

- `farmersdelight:backstabbing`
  - Book: start from Sharpness, make the blade shorter and angled like a knife/dagger.
  - Palette: steel blade, muted red accent, dark handle.
  - Glyph: small dagger silhouette angled down-left.

- `netherited:fireproof`
  - Book: start from Fire Protection, use a flame outline with a dark netherite core.
  - Palette: charcoal, orange, small purple/netherite highlight.
  - Glyph: shield or ingot with a tiny flame blocked by a bar.

## Legacy Or Optional Targets

- `magnetic_enchantment:magnetic`
  - Book: start from Loyalty or Channeling, replace the symbol with a horseshoe magnet.
  - Palette: red/blue magnet tips with gray metal center.
  - Glyph: tiny U magnet with one blue and one red pixel.

- `smelting_enchantment:smelting`
  - Book: start from Fire Aspect, add a small furnace mouth or molten ingot.
  - Palette: orange/yellow core, dark gray furnace outline.
  - Glyph: ingot over flame.

- `timber_enchantment:timber`
  - Book: start from Sweeping Edge or Efficiency, use axe plus log rings.
  - Palette: bark brown, tan rings, steel axe edge.
  - Glyph: axe head beside a 2x2 log ring.
