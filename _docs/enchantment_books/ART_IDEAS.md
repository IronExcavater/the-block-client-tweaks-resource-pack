# Enchantment Book And Glyph Ideas

## Active Targets

- `veinminer_enchantment:veinminer`: ore cluster and cyan vein cracks; must not read as Efficiency.
- `smelting_enchantment:smelting`: pickaxe plus hot ingot/flame; no sword imagery.
- `magnetic_enchantment:magnetic`: clear U-shaped red/blue magnet.
- `fallingtree:chopper` / `timber_enchantment:timber`: axe plus log rings/tree-felling motif.
- `netherited:fireproof`: dark ingot/shield blocking flame.
- `nova_structures:aerials_bane`: airborne/wing/updraft damage motif, not a skull.
- `nova_structures:antidote`: chestplate protected from poison/wither, not just a plain chestplate.
- `nova_structures:illagers_bane`: illager head with long nose/banner cue.
- `nova_structures:spiteful`: low-health retaliation / anger mark, visually distinct from Thorns.

## Notes

- `enchantments_modded.png` is the single compact 9px glyph sheet for all modded enchantments.
- `effects_modded.png` is the 10px glyph sheet for modded potion effects.
- Keep file names plain (`aerials_bane.png`) even when the real enchantment ID remains `nova_structures:aerials_bane`.
- Do not copy vanilla/Re-covered book art directly without a visible custom motif.
